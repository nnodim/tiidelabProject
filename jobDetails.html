Fullstack Developer

Fliqpay Technologies

Abuja, Nigeria

Full-time

Job posted: 1d ago

About:

Fliqpay is a leading provider of payment technology solutions. We are a dynamic and innovative tech company dedicated to providing the best payment solutions. We're on the lookout for a talented Full Stack Developer to join our growing team. If you are passionate about creating cutting-edge applications and thrive in a collaborative environment, we want to hear from you!

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of experience in JavaScript
  • 3+ years of experience in React
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.
  • Strong understanding of back-end technologies, including but not limited to Node.js, Python, Ruby, or Java.
  • Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases.
  • Experience with version control systems such as Git.
  • Experience in designing and developing RESTful APIs.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop new features and enhancements to existing applications.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Identify opportunities for process improvements and automation.
  • Write well-designed, testable, efficient code.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Flexible working hours
  • Remote work
